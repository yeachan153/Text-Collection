    \noindent  The hashtable can be accessed as follows. Enter the word you
are interested in as the key, for example: hash1{[}'and'{]}. This will
bring up a list of counters, how many counters there are depends on how
many documents there were. If you wish to see how many times 'and'
appeared in a particular document number, simply add an index after
entering your key (zero-indexed). For example, hash1{[}'and'{]}{[}0{]}
will give you the count of 'and' in your first document.
